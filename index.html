Research

  • Paper on Evaluating MEDLINE: Exploring the mechanisms responsible for variances across platforms accepted to EAHIL in Łódź, Poland. With Robert Shapiro, Tyler Nix, and Dr. Jeffrey Huber. June 2020.
  • Paper on Bibliographic Subject Analysis of Fake News accepted to QQML 2020 Barcelona, Spain. With Dr. Jenny Bossaller. May 2020.
  • Internal grant awarded for fake news project for $4,000. With Dr. Renee Kaufmann and Dr. Anthony Limperos. February 2020.
  • MEDLINE paper, Search result outliers among MEDLINE platforms, published in https://doi.org/10.5195/jmla.2019.622 with Robert Shapiro, Tyler Nix, and Dr. Jeffrey Huber. 2019.
