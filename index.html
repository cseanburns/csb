Research Updates

I am working on an information retrieval project related to bibliographic and abstracting/indexing databases. Other recent activities include:

  • Robert Shapiro, Tyler Nix, Jeff Huber and I had a talk accepted by the upcoming Medical Library Association conference. The title of the talk is Variation and Outliers in Search Results among MEDLINE-based Databases: A Longitudinal Study. The project is part of the MEDLINE research project mentioned below.
  • Robert Shapiro, Tyler Nix, Jeff Huber and I had a poster accepted at the 2019 iConference. The project examines discrepancies among MEDLINE databases. The title of the poster is Which MEDLINE? Outlier Search Results are Normal among MEDLINE Databases.
  • I am co-leading a workshop at the 2019 iConference with Marie Radford, Vanessa Kitzie, Diana Floegel, Lynn Silipigni Connaway, and Jenny Bossaller. The title of the workshop is InVivo Inspiration: Investigating Computer Assisted Qualitative Data Analysis Software (CAQDAS). I will be discussing RQDA, the qualitatative data analysis software that is part of the R programming ecosystem.
