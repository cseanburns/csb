Updates

I am working on an information retrieval project related to bibliographic and abstracting/indexing databases. Other recent activities include:

  • Chuck Fox and I concluded our three year project on bias in peer review with a publication in Scientometrics, volume 113, issue 2. Our previous work on peer review largely focused on gender bias, but this work focused on language and socioeconomics.
  • Jenny Bossaller, Amy VanScoy, and I published some work on qualitative secondary analysis as a methodology. This can be found in the Journal of Documentation, volume 73.
  • Chuck Fox, Anna Muncy, Jennifer Meyer, and I published our final paper on gender bias in peer review as it related to author suggested reviewers. The paper was published in Functional Ecology, volume 31.
