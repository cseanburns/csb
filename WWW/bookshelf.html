Bookshelf

Reading books is important, and thus, welcome to my bookshelf, where I keep track of what I read in my leisure time. I've enjoyed most of these books, especially the fiction works. Some of the nonfiction stuff I could have skipped (I'm specifically thinking of works I read in 2022), but I'll still learn something from them. What I mostly learn from nonfiction is that in most cases, I'd should almost always have chosen scholarly works to read instead.
