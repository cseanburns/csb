What's This About

It usually starts with an idea I have about some topic, and after we've worked through the idea a bit, I'll ask ChatGPT to write an essay based on our exchange. Below is a list of essays I've asked ChatGPT to write based on ideas I've had and our conversations.

While ChatGPT wrote these essays (and their titles), the ideas are mine and were flushed out through conversation.
